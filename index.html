Who We Are

Children's Cancer Institute is the only medical research institute in Australia wholly dedicated to curing childhood cancer and pioneering safer treatments for children. 60 years ago, childhood cancer was a death sentence. Today, 8 out of 10 children will survive. But there is much work to be done. We fund clinical research, provide clinical care and family support for family in need.

Learn More

Our Impact

Children’s Cancer Institute has been researching childhood cancer for more than 35 years and has become an internationally recognised leader in the field. Our science is world-class, and our research has made an contribution to improve understanding of cancer in children. What’s more, we have track record in research discoveries that impact on children’s treatment and survival.
